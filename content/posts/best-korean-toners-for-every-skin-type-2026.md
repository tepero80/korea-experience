However, in the Korean skincare philosophy, a toner (often called "skin" or "softener" in Korea) is the first liquid hydration step. In 2026, the technology has advanced significantly. We are seeing a move away from simple water-based formulas to "essence-toners"â€”viscous, nutrient-dense liquids that deliver active ingredients like peptides, Centella Asiatica, and fermented extracts deep into the epidermis.

Most top-tier toners now boast a slightly acidic pH level between 5.0 and 6.0, mirroring the skin's natural balance to prevent irritation.
