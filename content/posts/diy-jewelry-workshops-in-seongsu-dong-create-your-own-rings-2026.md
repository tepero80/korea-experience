## Why Seongsu-dong is the hub for DIY jewelry in 2026

Seongsu-dong has firmly established itself as the "Brooklyn of Seoul," transforming from a grit-heavy industrial shoe-making district into the epicenter of youth culture, pop-up stores, and artisanal craftsmanship. As of early 2026, there are over 40 registered jewelry workshops within a 1.5-kilometer radius of the Seongsu subway station, making it the densest concentration of craft studios in the capital.

The neighborhood's industrial heritage provides the perfect backdrop for metalworking. Unlike the polished commercialism of Gangnam, Seongsu workshops often inhabit renovated red-brick factories or second-floor spaces with exposed concrete, offering an aesthetic that is highly Instagrammable.

### The importance of 925 Sterling Silver
Most workshops in Seongsu use **925 Sterling Silver** (92.5% silver, 7.5% copper) or **99.9% Pure Silver**. Sterling silver is the industry standard for jewelry because pure silver is too soft for daily wear. The copper alloy adds the necessary hardness while maintaining the precious metal's luster. If you have severe metal allergies, ask specifically for 99.9% silver (often called "Sun-eun" in Korean), though be aware it dents more easily.

## Design trends 2026: What's hot in Seongsu?

Fashion in Seongsu moves fast. In 2024, highly polished, simple bands were the trend. Moving into 2026, the aesthetic has shifted towards **"Battered" or "Hammered" textures** (Tsuchime style) and **organic, irregular shapes**.

*   **Matte Finishes:** Instead of a mirror shine, a scratched or brushed finish is preferred for a modern, understated look.
*   **Open Rings:** Adjustable sizes are becoming popular as they are easier to gift.
*   **Fingerprint Engraving:** Some premium workshops offer laser scanning of your actual fingerprint to be engraved on the inside or outside of the ring.

If you are creating these as couple rings, know that this is a huge part of Korean dating culture. Couples often celebrate their "100-day anniversary" with rings.
