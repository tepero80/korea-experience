## The Gangnam Hub: Trade Center Store (COEX)

The Trade Center Store is the logistic favorite for business travelers. It is physically connected to the COEX Convention Center, the Grand InterContinental Hotel, and the City Airport Terminal. This makes it the most convenient option for duty-free shopping right before a flight.

### Connectivity and Duty-Free
The 8th, 9th, and 10th floors house the **Hyundai Department Store Duty Free**. Here, you can shop for international brands in USD without paying tax, provided you have your passport and flight ticket. The goods are then picked up at the airport gate. ## Practical Information for Travelers

### Strollers and Lockers
Hyundai Department Store offers excellent amenities for families and travelers. 
*   **Strollers:** High-end strollers (often brands like Stokke or Bugaboo) are available for free rental at the information desk on the 1st floor.
*   **Lockers:** Free lockers are available, usually near the subway connection entrances or the customer service centers. At The Hyundai Seoul, there is a dedicated "Luggage Storage" room for larger suitcases, which is a lifesaver if you are heading straight to the airport.

### Membership and VIP
While full VIP status requires annual spending over â‚©40,000,000, international visitors can sign up for the **K-Card** or Hyundai Department Store Global Membership. This is often done instantly with a passport. Benefits include:
*   5% discount coupons (brands exclude Hermes, Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Cartier).
*   Two free drink coupons per visit for the "Cafe H" lounge (usually Americano or Iced Tea).

## Comparisons: Is Hyundai Right for You?

To help you decide if Hyundai is the right fit for your shopping style compared to other giants like Shinsegae Centum City or Lotte World Tower, consider the vibe.

**Hyundai is for:** The modern traveler who values aesthetics, comfort, and trends. It is for the shopper who wants to buy a Loewe bag, visit a quirky pop-up art show, and eat at a Michelin-plate restaurant all under one roof without feeling claustrophobic.

**Shinsegae is for:** The classicist. If you want the deepest inventory of watches and jewelry and prefer a marble-clad, traditional luxury environment, Shinsegae Main Store or Gangnam is the choice.

**Lotte is for:** The efficiency seeker. If you want to buy bulk cosmetics, ginseng, and duty-free goods in Myeongdong and have zero interest in "relaxing" at the mall, Lotte is the most practical option.
