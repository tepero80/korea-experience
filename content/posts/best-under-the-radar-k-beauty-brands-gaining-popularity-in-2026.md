## Ingredient trends to watch in 2026

When scanning ingredient lists, look for these three powerhouses that are defining the 2026 market.

1.  **PDRN (Polydeoxyribonucleotide):** Derived from salmon DNA. It is excellent for wound healing and anti-aging. Formerly an injectable, now found in serums by brands like **IOPE** and **Genie the Bottle**.
2.  **Succinic Acid:** The 2026 replacement for Salicylic Acid. It is gentler on acne-prone skin but equally effective at controlling sebum.
3.  **Heartleaf (Houttuynia Cordata):** While popular in 2024, it has reached peak saturation in 2026. It is the go-to for soothing redness.
