## Seasonal Considerations: When to Go

Korea has four distinct seasons, and each offers a radically different glamping experience.

*   **Spring (April-May):** The most popular season. The cherry blossoms in Gapyeong are spectacular. Daytime temps are 15-20¬∞C, perfect for hiking.
*   **Summer (July-August):** Hot and humid (30¬∞C+). You **must** ensure your luxury glamping unit has powerful air conditioning. The pools will be the highlight.
*   **Autumn (September-November):** The "Golden Season." The foliage turns red and orange. This is peak "Bulmeong" season. Book 2 months in advance.
*   **Winter (December-February):** For the brave and romantic. Snow-covered landscapes are magical, and the contrast between the freezing air and the heated floor is cozy. Prices often drop by 30% in winter, except for Christmas.
