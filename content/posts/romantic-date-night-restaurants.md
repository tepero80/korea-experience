## 5. Romantic Hanok Dining: Traditional Atmosphere

For a uniquely Korean date night, dining inside a *Hanok* (traditional Korean house) is unforgettable. The architectural style dates back to the 14th century, featuring wooden beams (*seokkarae*) and tiled roofs (*giwa*).

*   **Proximity:** 50 meters from the palace entrance.

### Payment Methods & Tipping
A common question regarding high-end dining is tipping. In South Korea, **tipping is not required and can sometimes be refused.** The price you see on the menu includes a 10% VAT.

*   **Credit Cards:** Visa and Mastercard are accepted at 99.9% of restaurants. Amex is accepted at about 85% of premium venues but may struggle at smaller cafes.
*   **Apple Pay:** Availability is growing (currently around 20-30% of merchants), but do not rely on it. Always carry a physical card.

<InfoBox type="tip" title="Payment Pro Tip">
If you wish to pay for the meal discreetly without your date noticing, hand your credit card to the server or host *before* the meal begins. Say "Seon-gyeol-je" (pre-payment). This is a classy move often appreciated in Korean dating culture.
</InfoBox>

### Getting There: Transport Logistics
Seoul's traffic can be brutal, especially on Friday and Saturday evenings between 17:00 and 19:00. A 10-kilometer journey across the Han River can take 60 minutes by car.

*   **Subway:** The most reliable method. Most venues listed are within 500-800 meters of a station.
*   **Kakao T:** The Uber equivalent. Download this app before arrival. "Kakao Black" or "Venti" offers premium cars (Mercedes/Carnival) which are better suited for a dressed-up date night than standard taxis.

## 7. Dietary Restrictions and Special Requests

Korea has historically been difficult for vegetarians or those with gluten allergies, but the fine dining scene is adapting rapidly.

*   **Vegetarian/Vegan:** Temple food restaurants like *Balwoo Gongyang* (near Insadong) offer Michelin-star quality vegan meals. For the restaurants listed above, Jungsik offers a dedicated vegetarian tasting menu if requested 24 hours in advance.
*   **Allergies:** Always state allergies in the "Request" box on CatchTable. Korean kitchens take nut and shellfish allergies very seriously when notified.

## Conclusion: Making the Choice

Selecting the perfect restaurant comes down to the vibe you want to project. For impressive, world-class gastronomy, head to **Gangnam (Cheongdam-dong)**. For a romantic, moody, and artistic atmosphere, choose **Hannam-dong**. For history and culture, stick to **Jongno (Bukchon/Insadong)**.

Regardless of where you choose, the key to a successful date night in Seoul is preparation. Download CatchTable, map out your route to avoid rush hour traffic, and dress to impress. Seoul is a city that rewards effort, and a well-planned evening here will rival any romantic experience in Paris or Tokyo.
