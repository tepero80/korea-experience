## Ingredients to Look For (and Avoid)

When scanning labels in 2026, you will see English ingredients lists on almost all packaging.

**Look For:**
*   **Centella Asiatica (Cica):** The king of soothing. If your face is red or irritated, get this.
*   **Niacinamide:** Brightens skin and controls oil. 2% to 5% concentration is ideal.
*   **Hyaluronic Acid:** Binds water to skin. Look for "multi-molecular" for better absorption.
*   **Panthenol:** Strengthens the skin barrier.

**Avoid:**
*   **High Alcohol Content:** Many old-school men's toners are full of denatured alcohol to give a "cooling" sensation. This dries out the skin and causes more oil production long-term.
*   **Menthol/Peppermint:** Can be irritating for sensitive skin, though it feels refreshing.

## Seasonal Considerations

Korea has four distinct seasons, and your skin needs change with them.
*   **Summer (June-August):** Hot and humid (90% humidity). Switch to "Gel" type moisturizers and waterproof sunscreens.
*   **Winter (December-February):** Freezing and dry. Switch to "Cream" type moisturizers and add a lip balm.
