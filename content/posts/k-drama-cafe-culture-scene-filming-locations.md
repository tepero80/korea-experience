## Cafe Etiquette for Tourists

While Korea is very welcoming, there are unwritten rules regarding cafe usage, especially in popular filming locations where turnover is high.

1.  **One Drink Per Person:** This is strictly enforced. Even if you are not thirsty, you must order a drink to occupy a seat. Sharing one cake does not count toward this requirement.
2.  **Time Limits:** Popular spots may have a 2-hour seating limit during peak weekends.
3.  **Photography:** It is acceptable to take photos, but avoid photographing other customers. Influencer-style shoots that disrupt the flow of service may be stopped by staff.
4.  **Wi-Fi & Power:** 95% of cafes offer high-speed gigabit Wi-Fi. Look for the password on the receipt or a small standee at the pickup counter.

## The Future of K-Drama Cafes: What to Expect in 2026

As of 2026, the trend is shifting towards "Concept Immersion." Cafes are no longer just offering coffee; they are offering experiences. Pop-up cafes related to specific drama releases are becoming common in the Seongsu-dong area (often called the Brooklyn of Seoul). These pop-ups often last only 2 to 4 weeks, coinciding with a show's finale.
