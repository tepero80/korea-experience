## Essential Amenities for a Recovery Hotel

When booking your accommodation, do not just look at the star rating. In 2026, specific amenities define a "Recovery Ready" room.

### 1. Adjustable Bedding & Pillows
After rhinoplasty or facial contouring, you must sleep elevated (at a 45-degree angle) for at least 7 to 14 days to reduce deswelling. Hotels like **Andaz** and **Josun Palace** offer a "pillow menu."
*   **Action:** Email the hotel in advance to request 4 extra firm pillows.

### 2. Humidifiers
Korea can be very dry, especially in winter and spring. Dry air can irritate the throat (sore from intubation) and dry out the eyes (after blepharoplasty).
*   **Pro Tip:** Most 5-star hotels in Gangnam have humidifiers available upon request, but stock is limited. Reserve one when booking.

### 3. Blackout Curtains
Your sleep cycle will be disrupted. You might need to sleep at 2 PM. High-quality blackout curtains are non-negotiable for getting the rest required for tissue regeneration.

## Safety Protocols and Emergency Access

In the rare event of a complication (excessive bleeding, high fever, extreme pain) outside of clinic hours, you need to know your surroundings.

*   **Severance Hospital (Gangnam):** One of the largest university hospitals with an International Health Care Center. Located near Dogok Station.
*   **Samsung Medical Center:** Located in Irwon-dong, specialized in emergency care.

All hotels listed above have protocols to call an ambulance (119) immediately. The 119 service has interpretation available.
