## FAQ

### Q1: Does Potenza really hurt less than Morpheus8?
**Yes, significantly.** It‚Äôs not painless, but it‚Äôs annoying rather than excruciating. Morpheus8 penetrates deeper and uses a coarser needle mechanism. If Morpheus8 is an 8/10 on the pain scale, Potenza is a solid 4/10 with numbing cream.

### Q2: Can I wear makeup right after?
**Technically yes, but please don't.** Your skin has thousands of open micro-channels (holes!). Putting makeup on is asking for clogged pores or infection. Wait at least 24 hours. Use the downtime to wear a mask and sunglasses like a K-pop star incognito.

### Q3: Why is it so much cheaper in Korea? Is it safe?
**It is safe.** It's cheaper because of *competition* and *volume*. There are 500+ clinics in Gangnam alone fighting for customers. Also, many of the devices (like Lutronic) are Korean-made or manufactured nearby, reducing overhead costs. The doctors perform these procedures 50 times a day‚Äîthey are experts.

### Q4: How long do the results last?
**Collagen isn't permanent.** Potenza results peak around 3 months and last about a year. Morpheus8 results can last 12-18 months because of the deeper structural remodeling. To keep it, you need maintenance sessions once a year.

### Q5: What is "Skin Stacking"?
**It's the 2026 trend.** Instead of just doing Potenza, clinics stack treatments in one session. Example: Potenza (for depth) + LDM (ultrasound for calming) + Exosomes (for healing). It maximizes the downtime window‚Äîyou heal from three things at once.
