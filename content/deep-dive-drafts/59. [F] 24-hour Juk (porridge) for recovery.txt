## STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

**Guide Title:** How to Get Healing Porridge at 3:00 AM in Seoul
**Total Time:** 15 Minutes
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Step 1: Locate the Nearest "Big" Convenience Store
Don't just go to the tiny kiosk in the subway station. Look for a GS25, CU, or 7-Eleven that looks reasonably sized from the street. The bigger the store, the wider the "Home Meal Replacement" (HMR) section will be.
> **Pro Tip:** If you see "Emart24," go there‚Äîthey often have their own premium private label brands that are surprisingly gourmet.

### Step 2: Navigate to the "Retort" Aisle
You are looking for shelf-stable pouches that stand upright, usually near the instant rice (Hetban) or canned goods. Look for the characters **Ï£Ω** (Juk). You will see brands like **Bibigo (ÎπÑÎπÑÍ≥†)** and **Yangban (ÏñëÎ∞ò)**.

### Step 3: Purchasing and Heating
Take your chosen pouch to the counter. If you want to eat it *now*, point to the microwave and ask "Microwave?" (or just gesture). Most pouches have a "steam vent" or require you to tear the corner slightly before heating.

## FAQ

### Q1: Is Bonjuk open 24 hours?
**No.** This is the biggest misconception. Bonjuk is a "slow food" franchise and operates standard restaurant hours, typically opening around 9:00 AM and closing by 9:00 PM or 9:30 PM. They are **not** a late-night drunken food spot.

### Q2: What is the best porridge for a hangover?
**Gimchi-Nakji Juk (Kimchi & Octopus Porridge).** It‚Äôs spicy, which makes you sweat out the toxins, and the octopus contains taurine, which is great for liver recovery. It wakes up your system. If your stomach is too sensitive for spice, go for Abalone Juk.

### Q3: What is the best porridge after plastic surgery?
**Hobakjuk (Sweet Pumpkin Porridge).** It is universally recommended by Korean surgeons because pumpkin is rich in potassium and Vitamin A, which helps the body reduce post-operative swelling (edema) significantly faster.

### Q4: Can I bring Juk on the plane home?
**Only if it's in a sealed pouch (retort) and in your CHECKED bag.** You cannot bring porridge in your carry-on because it is classified as a liquid/gel (over 100ml). Do not try to bring fresh restaurant Juk through security; they will throw it away.

### Q5: Is convenience store porridge healthy?
**It's decent.** While it has preservatives to stay shelf-stable, brands like Bibigo use real broth and grains. It is infinitely healthier than eating instant ramen (Ramyeon) at 3 AM because it‚Äôs lower in grease, easier to digest, and hydrating.
