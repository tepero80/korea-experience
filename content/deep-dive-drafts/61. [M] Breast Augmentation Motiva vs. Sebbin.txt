### Q1: Is it really cheaper to go to Korea for boobs in 2026 without the tax refund?

### Q2: Can I breastfeed with Motiva or Sebbin implants?
Generally, yes. Korean surgeons usually use the "Dual Plane" or "Under Muscle" method, which leaves the milk ducts intact. ### Q3: What happens if my implant ruptures when I'm back home?
Motiva has a global warranty (Always Confidence Warranty). If you have the chip (Ergonomix), they can scan it anywhere. ### Q4: Do I need to massage my breasts after surgery?

However, surgeons might prescribe "implant displacement exercises" (moving them gently) to keep the pocket open. Follow your specific doctor's orders, not Reddit!

### Q5: Will the airport security scanner beep at my boobs?
No, silicone doesn't set off metal detectors. However, if you have the Motiva implant with the Q-Inside microchip, it *is* an RFID chip, but it is designed not to set off standard airport security gates. It's only readable by a specific medical scanner. You're safe!
