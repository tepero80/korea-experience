## FAQ

### Q1: Is PDRN actually vegan?
No, absolutely not. True PDRN comes from salmon or trout sperm (milt). However, in 2026, some brands like **VT Cosmetics** utilize "Phyto-PDRN" derived from Panax Ginseng. It claims similar benefits, but if you want the "medical grade" repair backed by decades of studies, the fish DNA is still the gold standard.

### Q2: Does the Rejuran injection really hurt that bad?
I'm not going to lie to you‚Äîyes. It‚Äôs often called "the most painful skin booster." The liquid is thick, so it stings when it enters the skin. Most clinics offer "Rejuran HB Plus" now, which has lidocaine (numbing) mixed *inside* the syringe. **Pay extra for HB Plus.** Seriously. It reduces the pain by like 50%.

### Q3: Can I use Retinol with PDRN and Exosomes?
Yes! Actually, they are best friends. Retinol breaks down old skin cells (turnover), and PDRN/Exosomes help repair the barrier and reduce the irritation retinol causes. It‚Äôs a power couple. Just use the PDRN *before* the retinol to buffer the sensitivity.

### Q4: How long does Juvelook last compared to Filler?
Regular HA filler dissolves in 6-12 months. Juvelook (PLLA) stimulates your own collagen, which can last **1.5 to 2 years**. It‚Äôs a slower start‚Äîyou won‚Äôt see full results for about 6 weeks‚Äîbut the finish line is much further away.

### Q5: Is topical PLLA (creams) worth it?
It‚Äôs... okay. Topical PLLA can‚Äôt penetrate deep enough to create massive volume like an injection can. It acts more like a spackle, smoothing out very fine surface lines and hydrating. Don't expect it to lift sagging jowls, but it‚Äôs a nice finishing step for texture.
